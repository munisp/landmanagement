## Important Extracted Details

### Esri
- Frames modern land administration around three systems: **record**, **insight**, and **engagement**.
- Highlights cloud-based public information access, collaboration, property value analysis, and reducing inquiries/appeals through better apps and maps.

### Landfolio
- Claims strong support for **cadastre + registry unification**, a **single source of truth**, **clear chain of title**, **mortgages/liens/easements/encumbrances**, **audit trail**, and **cross-agency workflows**.
- Explicitly mentions reporting dashboards and map-based search / public access.

### Hexagon
- Highlights **mapping**, **visualization**, **analysis**, **public administration solutions**, and **interagency situational awareness**.
- Strong on geospatial and surveying / reality-capture capabilities.

### Tyler Technologies
- Strong emphasis on **records management**, **public transparency**, **AI**, **cybersecurity**, **vulnerability scanning**, and **data & insights** around public administration workflows.
- Suggests a broader operational maturity layer beyond basic land records alone.
